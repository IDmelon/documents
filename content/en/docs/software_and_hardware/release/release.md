## IDmelon Panel 2.16

April 21, 2026

**Bug Fixes**

- Login option for shared accounts not showing up during login attempts? Hopefully, we won't be seeing this issue anymore.
- Passkeys on the IDmelon Authenticator app for Android no longer perform magic tricks where they disappear, only to reappear after a refresh. Passkeys now stay visible at all times in your IDmelon app for Android.
