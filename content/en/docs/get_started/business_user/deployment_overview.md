## Step 1: Download the Necessary Software

1. Go to your smartphone's app store and download the [IDmelon Authenticator](https://idmelon.com/products/idmelonauth) app.

2. Download and install the [IDmelon pairing tool](https://idmelon.com/docs/downloads) on your PC.

## Step 2: Accept the Security Key Invite

1. Using your smartphone, open the activation email sent by IDmelon. Make sure to check on your junk folder. If you have not received anything, contact your organization's IT admin.

2. Tap on **Activate**. This will automatically activate your security key. Now, you should see a security key icon when you go to your IDmelon Authenticator app.

## Step 3: Pair Your Smartphone and Computer ([video tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9rFCvInykdo&ab_channel=IDmelonTechnologiesInc.))

1. Open IDmelon's Pairing Tool app on your computer, and IDmelon's Authenticator app on your phone.

2. Click on **Pair a New Smartphone** on the Pairing Tool, and the QR code icon on the top right of the smartphone app.

3. Scan the QR code shown on screen. Your Authenticator app should display a success message.

4. Click on **Next** on the Pairing Tool. You have successfully paired your phone's IDmelon application with your computer.

## Step 5: Use Your Security Key to Log In To Microsoft.

1. Go to <https://www.office.com/> and click sign in.

2. Select **Sign in Options**.

3. Select **Sign in With Security Key**.

4. Either select **use phone or tablet** and scan the QR Code with your IDmelon Authenticator. [This video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9nb4xU0GNC8&ab_channel=IDmelonTechnologiesInc.) shows using the QR Code.
