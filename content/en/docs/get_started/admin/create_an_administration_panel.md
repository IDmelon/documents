1. Click [here](https://panel.idmelon.com) to create your organization’s admin panel.
2. Select Work or School on behalf of your organization.
3. Fill in all the fields with your information, including your email address and phone number
4. Enter your company's URL and Name. You can also add your company's logo.
5. Choose the device you would like to use as your security key (recommended), or **Skip** it for later.

To activate your security key, based on your selected device, please follow one of the following sections:

1. Smartphone  
   a. Choose **Smartphone** as your security key device.  
   b. Use your smartphone camera or, on your smartphone, use the [IDmelon Authenticator](https://idmelon.com/products/idmelonauth) app to scan the QR Code shown on your screen. If scanning from the IDmelon Authenticator app, select the three-line menu icon in the top-left corner and **Scan QR Code** or **Add New Security Key**.
   c. Click next and either download the [IDmelon Pairing Tool](https://idmelon.com/docs/downloads) or use your [IDmelon Reader](https://idmelon.com/products/reader) to log in to your panel using your smartphone as your security key.  
2. Contactless Card  
   a. Choose **Contactless Card** as your security key device.  
   b. Based on your card reader model (e.g., rfIDEAS, HID, phg) and operating system, download and install the corresponding latest [Card Reader Driver](https://idmelon.com/docs/downloads).
   c. Enter a name for your security key device and click on **Set up your Contactless Card**.  
   d. Quickly tap your card on your card reader to register your card. Most readers will either make a noise or flash their light after successfully reading a card.
   e. Click on **Done** to receive your PIN via email.
   > Note: The received PIN will serve as a verification method during login with your contactless card. This can be modified or disabled later on.
3. FIDO2.0 Hardware Security Key  
   a. Choose **FIDO2 Hardware** Security Key as your security key device.  
   b. Enter a name for your hardware security key and click on **Register**.  
   c. Touch your Hardware Security key.
