IDmelon hybrid readers allow for users to quickly log in and log out of their apps in shared iOS devices, allowing users to get to work faster and more securely.

This short document will guide you through the steps to get your IDmelon hybrid reader up and running in a few quick steps.

## What You'll Need
Before getting started, make sure you have the following:
1. An **IDmelon hybrid reader**.
2. A **power source** for the reader.
3. An **iOS device** with:
    - Bluetooth enabled.
    - The **IDmelon Authenticator app** installed.
    - The app configured in [Shared Device mode](/docs/for_administrators/shared_devices/shared_mobile_devices/shared_device_mode_configuration_ios_ipad/).

## Setup and First Use

Once your hardware is ready, follow these steps to start using the hybrid reader:

1. **Connect your reader to power.**

    Plug the IDmelon hybrid reader into a power source. It may take up to one minute to fully start. 

    The reader is ready when you see a red light on the card reader and the IDmelon dongle is lit.
2. **Place the reader nearby.**

    Keep the reader close enough to the iOS device so it can connect properly over Bluetooth.     
3. **Sign in.**
    a. Open the **IDmelon Authenticator** app.
    b. Tap your badge on the reader.
    c. Enter your PIN.
    d. Tap **Next** when prompted by the Entra sign-in screen.
4. **Sign out when finished**

    When you're done, tap your badge on the reader again to securely sign out of all connected services.
